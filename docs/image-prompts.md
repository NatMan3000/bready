## Tips for Consistency

- Use the same aspect ratio (1:1 square works well for cards)
- Add "warm amber color temperature" or "golden hour lighting" to prompts
- Include "rustic wooden cutting board" in every prompt
- Avoid pure white backgrounds - keep everything in warm tones
- Request "shallow depth of field" for a professional look
